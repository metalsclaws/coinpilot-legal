Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Coinpilot ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our use of the TikTok API and related services.

1. Who We Are

Coinpilot is a personal finance and AI tools content account operated as a solo creator project. Our content is published on Instagram (@coinpilot.ai) and TikTok (@coinpilot.ai).

2. Information We Access

Through the TikTok Content Posting API, we access the following data solely for the purpose of publishing our own content:

  • TikTok account credentials (access tokens) to authenticate posting requests
  • Basic account information (username, user ID) to verify the correct account is being used

We do not collect, store, or process data from any third-party TikTok users or viewers.

3. How We Use This Information

The data accessed via the TikTok API is used exclusively to:

  • Publish video content to our own TikTok account (@coinpilot.ai)
  • Verify that our automated posting pipeline is functioning correctly

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

API access tokens are stored locally on the operator's own machine. They are not transmitted to any external servers beyond TikTok's own API endpoints. Tokens are refreshed periodically and revoked when no longer needed.

5. TikTok's Own Privacy Policy

Our use of the TikTok platform is also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy. We do not override or circumvent TikTok's own data handling practices.

6. Your Rights
